QMy 9-year-old has an auditory score of 8.6 on NeuroSense. What accommodations should I request at school?
Request preferential seating away from high-noise areas (doors, HVAC, hallways). Ask for written instructions alongside verbal ones. Noise-canceling headphones should be on the IEP as a permitted accommodation. A quiet workspace for tests and focused work is also your right to request under Section 504.
Dr. Priya Nair, Pediatric OT · 12 years experience  ·  Answered Nov 16
QHow long does it typically take to see improvement with a sensory diet?
Most families see meaningful changes within 4–8 weeks of consistent implementation. Sensory diets work on the nervous system's neuroplasticity — daily, repeated input gradually shifts tolerance thresholds. Consistency matters more than perfection.
Dr. Vikram Singh, Developmental Pediatrician  ·  Answered Nov 14
